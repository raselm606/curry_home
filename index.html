CurryChef Yield Farming Event

Auction Lobby

CurrySwap Auction Lobby

CurryChef Yield Farmers will be able to take their CURRY tokens and stake them in the Food Vault.

Each and every day, a pool of Curry tokens is auctioned off in exchange for ETH. This ETH is then taken and distributed as a dividend amongst users staking in the Food Vault.

The more Curry a user accumulates during the yield farm event, the more ETH they can passively earn!

Products

CurryChef Yield Farm

The CurryChef Yield Farm is a yield farming event whereby Curry tokens are minted and distributed. Deposit Uniswap LP tokens into the farm and earn Curry tokens. The yield farm is open for a limited time, and closes entirely with the launch of the Food Vault auction lobby.

Static Staking Pool

Our Static Staking pool allows users to stake Curry tokens directly into a pool and earn a higher APY compared to other staking pools. A higher block reward percentage is allocated to this pool, and is perfect for accumulating even more Curry rewards during the yield farming event

Food Vault

The CurrySwap Food Vault functions similarly to the TRON-based T2X Staking Portal, except it is hosted on the Ethereum blockchain. This means after launch, users will be able to take their accumulated CURRY tokens from the yield farm and earn ETH dividends over time by staking them in the Food Vault.

NFT AIRDROP REWARDS

Limited edition CurrySwap NFT tokens will be rewarded to users staking CURRY tokens. These NFT tokens will be distributed via OpenSea.io. CurrySwap NFT tokens will have a variety of unique properties that make them special, such as only being available on a certain day or after a certain period of time staking CURRY tokens.
